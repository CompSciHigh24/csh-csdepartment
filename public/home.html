Cs department

The Computer Science Department at Comp Sci High is committed to empowering the next generation of Computer Scientists and technologists. Our department offers a rigorous curriculum that is designed to provide students with strong foundational skills in various coding principles and practices. Technology has historically marginalized people of color and we aim to build up our students to not only navigate, but also to transform the spaces they are in..From introductory to Advanced Placement courses, our program is tailored to the diverse interests of our students. Our dedicated faculty bring invaluable industry experience and academic expertise to the classroom, ensuring that students receive a high-quality Computer Science education.
